This website focuses on empowerment of unpriviledged,

by bringing helping hands together to help the vulnerable ones

with the help many do good NGO’s and the constant

support of the customers.

We don’t deal with money, all you have to do is buy a product

and we will send it to the NGO’s working with us

and they in turn supply these to the needy.

SERVICES

This website provides a smooth interface for usage and the products displayed will be the same outside as well, you can track your order till it reaches the person who needs it and the NGO’s will update the product which is needed the most, creating the ease of product selection.

PROJECTS

We support all the projects taken up by the NGO’s and will support them to the fullest, all the projects will be updated in the website with at most transparency.

CLIENTS

As of now we have NGO’s working with us, we seek to expand the network chain to support and help as many people as possible.
